---
title: "The beauty of constraints"
date: "2024-02-28"
excerpt: "How limitations can lead to better, more intentional design decisions."
---

Unlimited choice often produces mush. A tight constraint—format, palette, deadline, or platform—forces you to commit and iterate in a direction.

Constraints are a creative prompt. They rule out lazy solutions and make the remaining options easier to compare on merit.

Ship something small and coherent; expand once the core feels right. The best systems grow from a kernel that already works.
