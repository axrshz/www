---
title: "Building for focus"
date: "2024-03-08"
excerpt: "Designing products that respect your attention and time."
---

Attention is finite. Software that interrupts without good reason trains people to tune out—not just your app, but everything on the device.

Focus-friendly design starts with defaults: quiet notifications, clear primary actions, and paths that do not require hunting through dense menus.

Respect also means honest latency. If something takes time, show real progress instead of fake optimism. Predictability builds trust faster than polish alone.
